Taj Mahel

The Mughal king Shah Jahan (1592-1666) built the Taj Mahal, a mausoleum of white marble, in memory of his favorite wife Mumtaz Mahal (1593-1631).It was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan to house the tomb of his favorite wife, Mumtaz Mahal. It represents the excellence of Mughal architecture.

The Great Wall of China

The Great Wall developed from the disparate border fortifications and castles of individual Chinese kingdoms. For several centuries these kingdoms probably were as concerned with protection from their near neighbours as they were with the threat of barbarian invasions or raids.
